About The Project

Revolutionizing Urban Living Through IoT

Our Smart City Dashboard represents a transformative approach to urban management, harnessing cutting-edge IoT technology to create safer, more efficient, and sustainable city infrastructure.

  • Real-Time Urban Monitoring – IoT-powered dashboard tracks city infrastructure 24/7
  • Automated Efficiency – Smart systems optimize parking, traffic, and waste management
  • Safety-First Design – Integrated detectors and emergency alerts protect citizens
